Postdoctoral Position in MRI Physics Research


Postdoctoral position in MRI physics at Center for Biomedical Imaging Research (CBIR) is available immediately in Tsinghua University School of Medicine in Beijing, China.

CBIR is the key discipline of Tsinghua University supported by government funding. CBIR is equipped with a Philips 3T TX whole-body MRI scanner, which has parallel RF transmission. This system is totally research dedicated. CBIR is also equipped with affiliated equipment for imaging technique development, hardware development, image processing and clinical research.

Successful candidates will work with MRI physicists, physicians and psychiatriests. The research projects will primarily focus on MRI sequences, image reconstruction, 4D fast imaging, cardiovascular imaging, abdominal imaging and fMRI. The position is available immediately and application will be accepted until the position is filled. Competing payment is based upon experience.

Required Qualifications:
Completed Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering, Physics, Biomedical Engineering or other related field;
High motivation and interests in MRI research;
Proficient in C or C++ as well as Matlab or IDL;
Strong communication skills in both English and Chinese;
Research experience in MRI;
Development experience with the Philips Paradise (Goal-C) tool is a plus.

Please email a curriculum vitae, a research statement, and three recommendation letters to: huaguo at tsinghua.edu.cn. Please email us for on-site interview arrangement.

 

磁共振影像博士后

 清华大学生物医学影像研究中心（CBIR）成立于2010年6月。本中心配备一台专门用于磁共振成像方法开发的磁共振成像系统，具有开放的软、硬件开发环境，可以进行序列开发，图像重建和线圈开发。该中心现有8名研究员及多名工程师和工作人员，同时和国际知名磁共振物理学家有密切的合作，每年中心也会举行精彩的学术活动，如磁共振核心技术的系列讲座等。

中心的研究重点目前集中在磁共振物理和工程，包括序列设计，图像重建，图像的量化分析。 在应用方面，主要集中在心血管影像，肝部影像，功能磁共振，肿瘤影像等。

基于本中心研究的工作需要，现拟招募磁共振影像研究助理或博士后一名或多名。工作方向将集中于磁共振影像物理，成像方法，图像重建，算法开发，图像后处理研究。

资格要求：
1. 已获得电子工程学，物理学，数学，自动化，通信，生物医学工程学或相关领域的博士;
2.较强的英语和中文的交流与沟通能力；
3. 熟悉C 或 C++，以及 Matlab 或 IDL;
4. 有MRI科研经历;
5. 了解Philips Paradise (Goal-C)

工作待遇：
在清华大学博士后规定基础之上提供有竞争力的薪酬和待遇，包括解决北京户口、小孩上学、住房等。对于发表过有重要影响的科研论文的或在国外著名大学获得博士学位的申请者，我们将帮助其申请清华大学各类博士后基金（使其年薪可达RMB100,000以上）。

申请方式：
申请人请将以下材料发至 huaguo at tsinghua.edu.cn
1. 个人简历(中、英文)
2. 三封专家推荐信及推荐专家的联系方式
3. 简要的个人科研计划（中、英文）
邮件题目请标注：清华大学生物医学影像研究中心博士后

申请者资料课题组保密，恕不退还。
